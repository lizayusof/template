Main Research Topics

Grids of stellar evolution models

Computational astrophysics - calculating the grids of stellar masses with various metallicities. Currently we are working on grids of supersolar metallicity and focus on the evolution of massive stars.

Stellar neutrinos

In the evolution of massive stars, one of the important aspect to keep the star alive is through neutrino cooling/neutrino energy loss. Our research includes the nuclear and the thermal aspect of the neutrino production in massive and very massive stars.

Progenitors of black holes

When massive stars undergoes core collapse, one of the final product is the black hole.
